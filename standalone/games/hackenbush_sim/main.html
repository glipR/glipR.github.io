Creating Edges

Click and drag on the canvas to create a new edge, either on the black base-line or nodes at the top of edges.

To create Blue, Green or Red edges, use the left, middle and right mouse buttons, or the left mouse button holding the numbers 1, 2 and 3.

Navigating

You can hold shift and click elsewhere in the canvas to drag your view around. You can also scroll in and out from a point.

Cutting Edges

Edges can be cut by simply clicking and dragging your mouse across the edge (No check is made to see if this is within the rules)

Saving Games

If you want to save games with known values to use later, simply press Enter, or press the 'Save' button and give it a name.

To add it to your current game, drag it in from the right sidebar. Hold shift before dragging to invert the game.

Evaluating Games

Press the "Calculate Game" button to evaluate whether your game is a win for Red (R), Blue (B), the 1st (||) or 2nd (0) player.
